I've been scouring the internet for a complete list of wallets that are compatible with OpenAlias, but have been fruitless in my efforts of finding such a list which is updated. I'm hoping to make as complete of a list as possible of wallets that support OpenAlias in hopes to increase support for the protocol.

This page is actively maintained. Wallet support for OpenAlias may change over time — updates are welcomed on the GitHub.

Wallets with OpenAlias support

  • Cake Wallet (XMR, BTC, LTC)
  • Monero.com (XMR)
  • Coin Wallet (XMR, BTC, LTC)
  • Monero GUI/CLI (XMR)
  • Electrum (BTC)
  • Electrum-LTC (LTC)
  • FeatherWallet (XMR)
  • MyMonero (XMR)
